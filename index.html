About Fox Project

Fox Project is a long-term digital ecosystem designed to connect education, media and creative studios into one unified system.

It is not just a website - it is a structured universe of interconnected platforms, including Fox Edu Platform, Fox Games - Polish game studio, and Fox Media Studio. What makes it even more unique is that the entire ecosystem was created by a single person - a 12-year-old builder, designer, programmer and creator - who is developing all of these systems independently. This is not a collection of random projects. It is one cohesive digital world designed, built, and continuously expanded by one mind.

Vision

Fox Project aims to build a full digital universe where education, entertainment and media are part of one connected ecosystem.

Each studio evolves independently but follows the same philosophy: cinematic quality, modern design and long-term scalability.
